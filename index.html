⚠️ SCAM AWARENESS GUIDE

How To Recognize Online Security Scams

Online scams can appear in many forms, including fake messages, suspicious websites, misleading offers, and attempts to collect personal information. Learning common warning signs can help you make safer decisions and protect your digital identity.

Get Support Learn Safety Tips

Scam Prevention Checklist

  • ✔ Verify websites before entering personal details.
  • ✔ Avoid unexpected emails and suspicious links.
  • ✔ Never share passwords or sensitive information.
  • ✔ Check software sources before downloading.
  • ✔ Be careful with urgent security messages.
  • ✔ Use strong passwords and account protection.
  • ✔ Review privacy settings regularly.
  • ✔ Keep devices and applications updated.
Important Safety Reminder:

Always verify information through trusted sources. Scammers often use urgency, fear, or unrealistic promises to influence decisions. Staying informed is one of the best ways to improve online safety.

FAQ CENTER

Frequently Asked Questions

Learn how to recognize suspicious activity, improve security awareness, and follow safer online practices.

How can I identify a suspicious message?

Look for unusual requests, urgent language, unknown senders, and links that do not match trusted websites.

What should I do before downloading software?

Verify the source, review permissions, and make sure the download comes from a trusted provider.

Why do scammers use urgency?

Creating pressure can make people act quickly without checking important details.

How can I improve my online safety?

Use strong passwords, keep software updated, and stay informed about common online risks.
